International Conference

From Molecule to Ecosystem

Global Security through Sustainable Resource Management

Who Can Attend?

We invite researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and students to submit abstracts for oral and poster presentations. This conference bridges molecular innovations with ecosystem-scale strategies, fostering dialogue on sustainable resource management as a concrete pathway to global security and sustainability — making it attractive to both technical and interdisciplinary contributors.
